QR Code Pro - Scan & Generate QR Codes

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

Welcome to QR Code Pro! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Important: By using QR Code Pro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera Access

QR Code Pro requires access to your device's camera to scan QR codes. We do not store, transmit, or share any images or videos captured through the camera. Camera access is used solely for real-time QR code scanning functionality.

1.2 Photo Library Access

When you choose to scan a QR code from your photo library, we request temporary access to the selected image only. The image is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

1.3 QR Code Data

When you scan or generate QR codes, the content is processed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit the data contained within QR codes unless you explicitly choose to share it through the app's sharing features.

1.4 Generated QR Codes

QR codes you generate using our app are created and stored locally on your device. We do not have access to or store copies of your generated QR codes on our servers.

1.5 Device Information

We may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide QR code scanning and generation services
  • App Improvement: To understand how users interact with our app and improve user experience
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Analytics: To analyze app usage and performance

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

QR Code Pro uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This includes:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Usage data
  • Location data (if permitted)

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

3.2 Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated data with our advertising partners for analytics purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All QR code data, generated codes, and scan history are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw camera and photo library permissions at any time through device settings

6. Children's Privacy

QR Code Pro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Permissions

QR Code Pro requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan QR codes in real-time
  • Photo Library: To scan QR codes from saved images
  • Storage: To save generated QR codes to your device

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings. Note that revoking permissions may limit app functionality.

8. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over data retention. You can delete the app and all associated data at any time by uninstalling the application from your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. International Users

QR Code Pro is available globally. If you are using the app from outside Pakistan, please note that your information may be processed in Pakistan or other countries where our service providers operate.

11. Your Consent

By using QR Code Pro, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
